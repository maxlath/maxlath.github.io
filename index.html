Maxime Lathuilière

I'm a 27 years old human trying my best to walk through life with joy and freedom, while contributing to the transition toward a post-consumerist society. In this process, I have a particular focus on the toxicity of commercial propaganda and what could come to replace it.

Experimenting on those questions of reorganizing information on ressources, I'm currently the main contributor of inventaire.io, a webapp that let you do the inventory of your books to inform your network of which you can give, share, or sell. Citizens empowerment is the mission, libre software and open knowledge is the way!
