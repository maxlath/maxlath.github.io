Libre Software

aka Free Software

Software made open not only for the convenience of openess but for Freedom as an ideal (cf the Free Software Foundation definition). VRM is an extension of those principle of liberty in the sphere of commercial conversations. P2P-RM aim to continue this struggle for Freedom in the field of access to information on resources/products.
